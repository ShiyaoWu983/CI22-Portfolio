RED.

The goal of this project is to create an exhibition that shows all the red colors in our daily life. Talita began to collect things from home and recorded them. This exhibition will show the importance of red in people's daily life, and how this color affects people in different ways. Does it give you ambition? Or does it make you very upset? The website should be easy to follow up with a continuous transition to the next project. Every aspect should be red or the audience should be reminded of the red color.
